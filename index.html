Bio

He is a PhD student at the Computer Vision Research Group in University of Amsterdam, supervised by Dr. Shaodi You. His research interests mainly lie in Depth Sensing and Human Pose Estimation.
Prior to that, he received his master's degree in Electronic and Information Engineering from Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Xiu Li, and his bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Xi'an Jiaotong University.
