Bio

I am a Lecturer in Machine Learning at the Department of Computer Science, the University of Sheffield, UK. Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Imperial Computer Vision and Learning Lab of Imperial College London working with Dr. Tae-Kyun Kim and a Postdoctoral researcher at Department of Automation of Tsinghua University working with Prof. Jie Zhou and Prof. Jiwen Lu. I received my Ph.D. from Tsinghua University under the supervision of Prof. Jie Zhou and Prof. Jiwen Lu. My research interest lies in 3D vision on human - the perception of 3D humans by machine and large scale retrieval.

Working with me?

I am always looking for motivated PhD students to work with me on computer vision and machine learning. Funding is available on a competitive basis. For informal enquiries about the research or positions, please email me indicating your areas of interest and including your CV.
Collaboration and visiting are also welcome. Please drop me an email if you are interested in working with me.
